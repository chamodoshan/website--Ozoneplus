OUR PRODUCT

OzonePlus is a smart mask with lot of features to protect you from toxic gagses everytime.The main purpose of this product is to let the general public have access to the air quality measurements of the surroundings that they are living and to know about the impurities in the air and to know how to overcome them by using the mask at times only when needed. Most of the masks that are being used are to be worn at all times which becomes very much hard to be done when used for prolonged periods. As sweat builds up and its quite uncomfortable to wear a mask for a whole day. Our aim is reducing the amount of time that the users has to wear the mask if the conditions are favorable to humans. This would reduce your discomfort up to a certain amount.

Air pollution is one of the most serious problems in the world. It refers to the contamination of the atmosphere by harmful chemicals or biological materials. Lots of people affected by air pollution and those affects inherited to next generation too hence we made a device Ozone Plus that could help people to give warning when chosen gas achieved unsafe level. This device has two parts

1. The wearable mask

2. The stationery one with built in GPS that would the let the users of the app to know the polluted areas before entering them.

The main functions of the product is to identify the harmful gases and toxics, smoke and other sudden change of gas percentage variations and also there will be a temperature and humidity sensor to check the temperature of the surroundings.
